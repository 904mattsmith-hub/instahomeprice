America's oldest city, three modern markets

St. Augustine values don't follow one curve. Downtown and the historic core (32084) trade on character, walkability, and the realities of owning an older home — where age is an asset and a maintenance question at the same time. Across the Bridge of Lions, Anastasia Island and St. Augustine Beach (32080) price on beach access and island scarcity. Common questions

Frequently asked questions

How are historic district homes valued?

On two axes at once: the premium side (period character, lot history, walk-to-town location) and the practical side (roof, systems, insurance on an older structure). The report comps your home against other historic sales — never against new construction across town — and I make sure both axes show up in the number.

Is Anastasia Island really a separate market from downtown?

Completely. The island builds almost nothing new, and beach access plus that scarcity gives 32080 its own demand floor and its own comps. A downtown sale tells you very little about an island home's value — so your report won't lean on one.

Does World Golf Village comp against St. Augustine or Jacksonville?

Mostly against itself. The 210 corridor runs on builder pricing, amenity packages, and Jacksonville commute math — downtown sales barely touch it. Your report stays inside the corridor's comp set, where the truth is.
