Common questions

Frequently asked questions

How is this different from a Zestimate?

Instant estimates are built only from public records — square footage, tax data, past sales. The RVM® inside RPR® adds real MLS listing and sold data, and then I review the result against what's actually happening on your street. An algorithm has never walked a Jacksonville open house. I have.

What is an RPR report?

RPR® (Realtors Property Resource) is the national property database built for Realtors® by the National Association of Realtors. Your report includes the RVM® estimated value, comparable sales, and neighborhood market trends — prepared and sent by me personally.

What does it cost?

Nothing. The valuation is free, with no obligation — no listing agreement, no contract, no strings.

How fast will I get it?

Usually within one business day. Requests are pulled and reviewed every morning.

Do I have to list my home with you?

No. The numbers are yours either way. If you decide you want a deeper look, I'm happy to build a full comparative market analysis — but that's your call, never a condition.
